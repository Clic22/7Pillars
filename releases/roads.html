About This Release

Roads is a journey through our twenties to our thirties.


Trip captures the reckless energy of youth, tinged with nostalgia for moments we knew couldn't last.

Side confronts the weight of our first major mistakes—the ones that shape us, the scars we carry forward.

White explores the slow suffocation of routine. We didn't notice ourselves becoming prisoners of the everyday, walking the same paths without joy. But recognizing the trap creates hope—a chance to forge a new road, a new way forward.

End Of (instrumental) transforms that hope into rebirth. A new man emerges. A father.


From our twenties to our thirties, these are the roads we've walked. And this is the path we'll follow into the years ahead.
