About This Release

Part of the Roads EP. White explores the slow suffocation of routine—how daily life consumed us without us noticing. We became prisoners of the everyday, repeating the same patterns, walking the same paths without joy. But realizing we're trapped creates hope: a chance to forge a new road, a new way forward.
