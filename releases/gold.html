About This Release

In 2024, we both lost our grandmothers—our first experience grieving someone we loved deeply. This song is dedicated to our grandmothers.

Exploring grief through shifting perspectives—someone in their final days at a hospice and the grandchild they leave behind. Featuring echoing guitars, and electronic and acoustic drums, the track's deep lead vocals and backing harmonies create a bittersweet, rhythmic pulse.
