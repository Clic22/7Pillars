About This Release

"Blazing Star" blends pop seduction with funky, dancefloor-ready energy. Driven by a groovy bassline and a deep, flirtatious voice, it lands somewhere between The National, M, and Prince. Chosen by our community in a fan vote, it's the first of three singles coming out this year.
