About Us

Thomas

Aymeric

Our musical journey began in our early teenage years in Aymeric's basement, surrounded by guitars, bass, and drums.

From France, we've evolved from basement jam sessions to creating music in the cloud. After 15 years of friendship and musical exploration, we're bringing our alternative sound to the world through digital platforms.

"This is only the beginning of our journey as a music duo."
